Some interesting facts about cicadas.

  • You can eat cicadas. People bake, boil, broil, barbecue and saut√© them.
  • Although they are often referred to as locusts, they are not true locusts. Real locusts look like grasshoppers.
  • Only the males make the sound cicadas are famous for. Females respond by flicking their wings.
  • If you are using a power tool or lawn mower, a cicada may land on you. They confuse the noise for other cicadas.
  • Cicadas can damage young or weak trees. To protect trees, use netting.
  • They don't eat solid foods, but they do drink tree fluids.
  • Cicadas have five eyes.
